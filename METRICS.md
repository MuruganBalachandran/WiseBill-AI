# Product Metrics

## North Star Metric
**Total Identified Savings ($)**
- Why? It directly aligns with the user's core value proposition. The more money we identify as wasted, the more successful the tool is at its job, and the more likely the user is to convert/share.

## Input Metrics (Leading Indicators)
1. **Audit Completion Rate (%):** Visitors who land on the page vs. those who submit the form. (Measures friction).
2. **Viral Share Rate (%):** Percentage of completed audits that result in a click on the "Copy Link" or "Share on X" buttons. (Measures the strength of the viral loop).
3. **Lead Capture Conversion Rate (%):** Percentage of users who enter their email to download the PDF migration guide or book a consultation. (Measures business impact for Techvruk).
4. **AI Summary Fallback Rate (%):** Tracking how often the Anthropic API fails/times out, requiring the static fallback. (Measures system reliability).
