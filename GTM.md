# Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

## 1. The Core Loop (Viral Engineering)
WiseBill AI is designed around a single, powerful viral loop: **"Look how much money I just saved."**
1. **Trigger:** Founder/CTO runs an audit and discovers $2,000/yr in wasted AI spend.
2. **Action:** They share the dynamic Open Graph URL on Twitter/LinkedIn.
3. **Reward:** Social clout for being fiscally responsible + peer benchmarking validation.
4. **Acquisition:** Their network clicks the link (which hides PII but shows savings) and runs their own audit.

## 2. Acquisition Channels
- **Product Hunt Launch:** Positioned as "Mint for AI Teams." 
- **Engineering Leaders Communities:** targeted drops in CTO/VPE Slack groups (e.g., Rands Leadership, YC alumni networks). 
- **Content Marketing (The "Shadow AI" Narrative):** Publishing aggregated benchmark data ("The average YC startup wastes $1,320/yr on redundant Copilot/Cursor seats").

## 3. Techvruk Lead Handoff
- **Low Spend (<$100):** Add to newsletter.
- **Mid Spend ($100-$499):** Automated PDF migration checklist sent via email.
- **High Spend (>$500):** Prominent CTA to book a free optimization call with a Techvruk enterprise negotiator.
