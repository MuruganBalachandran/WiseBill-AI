# Unit Economics

## The Math Behind WiseBill AI

If Techvruk deployed this tool tomorrow, here is the unit economics breakdown for how this scales into a highly profitable lead-generation engine.

### 1. Value of a Converted Lead
- **Target Profile:** Series B startup, 100 employees. 
- **Techvruk Enterprise Offering:** Consolidating their AI stack and negotiating direct enterprise volume discounts. 
- **Estimated Contract Value (ACV):** Techvruk takes a 20% cut of the savings generated, or charges a flat $15,000/yr SaaS FinOps management fee. Let's assume a conservative **$15,000 ACV**.
- **LTV (3-year lifespan):** $45,000.

### 2. CAC at Each Channel
- **Organic Twitter/Threads:** CAC = $0 (pure sweat equity).
- **Hacker News Launch:** CAC = $0.
- **Direct Outbound (Emailing CFOs):** Assuming $200/mo for Apollo/Lemlist and 10 hours of SDR time ($300). Yields 10 booked meetings. CAC = **$50 per booked meeting**.

### 3. Conversion Funnel to Profitability
To break even on a $50 CAC, considering the $15k ACV, our funnel can be quite wide:
- **Landing Page Visit → Audit Completed:** 25% 
- **Audit Completed → Email Captured:** 15% 
- **Email Captured (Lead) → Consultation Booked:** 10%
- **Consultation → Closed Won:** 20%

*Math Check:* 
10,000 Visitors = 2,500 Audits = 375 Leads = 37 Consultations = **7 Closed Deals.**
7 Deals * $15,000 = **$105,000 in new ARR.**

### 4. Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months
To hit $1M ARR, we need approximately **67 enterprise customers** (at $15k ACV).
Using the funnel above, we need to generate:
- 67 / 0.20 = 335 Consultations
- 335 / 0.10 = 3,350 Captured Leads
- 3,350 / 0.15 = 22,333 Completed Audits
- 22,333 / 0.25 = **89,332 Total Unique Visitors.**

**What must be true to achieve this?**
1. The "Viral Board Slide" loop (PDF exports shared to VC boards) must achieve a viral coefficient of >0.15.
2. The pricing database (`PRICING_DATA.md`) must be kept flawlessly up to date, establishing total trust with finance leaders so they rely on the tool monthly.
3. Techvruk must successfully negotiate 20%+ discounts with OpenAI/Anthropic to justify their $15k ACV to the client.
